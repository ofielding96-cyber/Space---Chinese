The Sun
太阳
tài yáng
▼
A medium-sized star at the centre of our Solar System. The Sun generates energy through nuclear fusion, fusing hydrogen nuclei into helium in its core.
太阳是太阳系中心的中等大小恒星，通过核聚变将氢原子核聚合成氦，释放巨大能量。
★ Surface temp: ~5,500°C · Core temp: ~15,000,000°C
🔵
Nuclear Fusion
核聚变
hé jù biàn
▼
The process where two small nuclei combine to form a larger nucleus, releasing large amounts of energy. This is how stars produce their energy. Requires extremely high temperature and pressure.
两个较小的原子核结合形成一个较大的原子核，同时释放大量能量。这是恒星产生能量的方式，需要极高的温度和压力。
★ 4H → He + energy (mass is converted to energy: E=mc²)
🌌
Galaxy
星系
xīng xì
▼
A collection of billions of stars, gas, and dust held together by gravity. Our galaxy is the Milky Way. The observable universe contains over 2 trillion galaxies.
由数十亿颗恒星、气体和尘埃组成的集合体，由引力维系在一起。我们的星系是银河系。可观测宇宙中有超过2万亿个星系。
★ Milky Way 银河系 diameter: ~100,000 light-years 光年
🔴
Red-Shift
红移
hóng yí
▼
When a light source moves away from an observer, the wavelength of light appears stretched — shifted towards the red end of the spectrum. Distant galaxies show red-shift, proving the universe is expanding.
当光源远离观测者时，光的波长看起来被拉伸，向光谱的红端移动。遥远星系的红移现象证明宇宙正在膨胀。
★ More distant galaxy → greater red-shift → faster recession speed
💥
The Big Bang
大爆炸
dà bào zhà
▼
The current scientific model for the origin of the universe. ~13.8 billion years ago, all matter, energy, space and time began from an extremely hot, dense point. Evidence: red-shift of galaxies and cosmic microwave background radiation (CMBR).
宇宙起源的当前科学模型。约138亿年前，所有物质、能量、空间和时间从一个极热、极密的点开始。证据：星系的红移现象和宇宙微波背景辐射。
★ Evidence 证据: Red-shift 红移 + CMBR 宇宙微波背景辐射
🪐
Orbit
轨道
guǐ dào
▼
The curved path of an object around a star or planet, maintained by gravity. Planets orbit stars; moons orbit planets; satellites orbit Earth. Gravity is the force that keeps all orbiting objects on their curved path.
由引力维持的物体围绕恒星或行星的弯曲路径。行星围绕恒星运行，卫星围绕行星运行，人造卫星围绕地球运行。引力是使所有轨道天体保持弯曲路径的力。
★ Gravity keeps planets, moons and satellites in orbit 引力使行星、卫星保持轨道运行
⚫
Black Hole
黑洞
hēi dòng
▼
A region of space where gravity is so strong that nothing — not even light — can escape. Formed when a massive star collapses at the end of its life (after a supernova). Detected by their gravitational effects on nearby matter.
太空中引力极强的区域，连光也无法逃脱。由大质量恒星在生命末期（超新星爆发后）坍缩而形成。通过对附近物质的引力效应来探测黑洞。
★ Escape velocity > speed of light 逃逸速度 > 光速
📡
CMBR enrichment
宇宙微波背景辐射
yǔ zhòu wēi bō bèi jǐng fú shè
▼
📌 Enrichment — not named in the AQA Foundation spec, but useful for understanding the Big Bang. CMBR不在AQA Foundation规范中单独命名，但有助于理解大爆炸。
Cosmic Microwave Background Radiation: leftover thermal radiation from the Big Bang, now detected as faint microwave radiation coming from all directions in space.
宇宙微波背景辐射：大爆炸留下的热辐射，现在被探测为来自太空各个方向的微弱微波辐射。
★ Discovered 1964 by Penzias & Wilson — won Nobel Prize 诺贝尔奖
🌑
Dark Matter & Dark Energy
暗物质与暗能量
àn wù zhì yǔ àn néng liàng
▼
Dark matter is invisible matter that does not emit light but exerts a gravitational force — it appears to hold galaxies together. Dark energy is a mysterious force driving the ever-faster expansion of the universe. Both are still not fully understood.
暗物质是不发光但有引力效应的不可见物质，它似乎将星系聚合在一起。暗能量是一种推动宇宙以越来越快的速度膨胀的神秘力量。两者目前仍未被完全理解。
★ AQA spec point: "there is still much about the universe that is not understood" 宇宙中仍有许多未解之谜
💫
Nebula
星云
xīng yún
▼
A large cloud of gas (mainly hydrogen) and dust in space. Stars form when a nebula collapses under gravity — a process called accretion. The collapsed core heats up, eventually triggering nuclear fusion to form a protostar.
太空中由气体（主要是氢气）和尘埃组成的大云团。恒星在星云因引力坍缩时形成——这个过程称为吸积。坍缩的核心加热，最终触发核聚变，形成原恒星。
★ Nebula 星云 → Protostar 原恒星 → Main Sequence Star 主序星

Key Ideas to Describe
核心要点 — AQA Foundation: no equations needed for Space. You must be able to describe these ideas. 无需公式，只需描述这些概念。
✅ AQA Foundation Space Physics — This topic is entirely descriptive. There are no equations to recall. Focus on being able to explain the ideas below in your own words. 本章节完全是描述性的，没有需要记忆的公式。重点是能够用自己的语言解释以下概念。
⚖️  How Stars Stay Stable 恒星如何保持稳定
AQA 4.8.1.1 — The balance inside a main sequence star
INWARD FORCE 向内的力
Gravity pulls all the gas inward, trying to collapse the star.
引力将所有气体向内拉，试图使恒星坍缩。
⇌
OUTWARD PRESSURE 向外的压力
Nuclear fusion in the core releases energy, creating outward pressure.
核心的核聚变释放能量，产生向外的压力。
★ When these two forces are balanced, the star is stable — this is the main sequence stage. 当两种力平衡时，恒星处于稳定状态——即主序星阶段。
🌌  Red-Shift and the Expanding Universe 红移与宇宙膨胀
AQA 4.8.2 — What red-shift tells us
You need to be able to describe and explain these three linked ideas:

1. Light from distant galaxies is red-shifted — its wavelength is increased (stretched towards the red end of the spectrum).
来自遥远星系的光发生红移——波长增加（向光谱红端拉伸）。
2. The further away a galaxy is, the greater its red-shift — meaning it is moving away faster.
星系越遥远，红移越大——意味着它移动得越快。
3. This is evidence that space itself is expanding and supports the Big Bang theory.
这证明宇宙空间本身在膨胀，支持大爆炸理论。
💥  The Big Bang Theory 大爆炸理论
AQA 4.8.2 — Origin of the universe
The Big Bang theory states that the universe began from a very small, extremely hot and dense region. Since then, space has been expanding.
大爆炸理论指出宇宙从一个极小、极热、极密的区域开始，此后空间一直在膨胀。
KEY EVIDENCE 关键证据
🔴 Red-shift of distant galaxies shows the universe is expanding.
遥远星系的红移表明宇宙正在膨胀。 📡 Since 1998, observations of supernovae show distant galaxies are receding ever faster.
自1998年起，超新星观测表明遥远星系退行速度越来越快。
🔭  Orbits and Gravity 轨道与引力
AQA 4.8.1.3 — Natural and artificial satellites
Gravity provides the force that keeps planets, moons and satellites in their orbits.
引力提供使行星、卫星保持轨道运行的力。
🌍 Natural Satellites 天然卫星
Moons orbiting planets (e.g. our Moon orbits Earth).
围绕行星运行的卫星（如月球围绕地球）。
🛰️ Artificial Satellites 人造卫星
Man-made objects orbiting Earth (e.g. GPS, weather satellites).
围绕地球运行的人造物体（如GPS、气象卫星）。
⭐  Elements Made in Stars 恒星中产生的元素
AQA 4.8.1.2 — Fusion and element formation
Fusion processes in stars produce all naturally occurring elements up to iron. Elements heavier than iron (e.g. gold, uranium) are only produced during a supernova.
恒星中的核聚变产生铁以内的所有自然存在的元素。比铁更重的元素（如金、铀）只在超新星爆发时产生。
★ When a supernova explodes, it scatters these elements throughout the universe — this is why heavy elements exist on Earth! 超新星爆炸将这些元素散布到整个宇宙——这就是地球上存在重元素的原因！
🌑  Dark Matter & Dark Energy 暗物质与暗能量
AQA SPEC POINT
AQA 4.8.2 — What we don't yet understand 我们尚未理解的事物
The AQA spec states that there is still much about the universe that is not understood. Two key mysteries are:

Dark Matter 暗物质 — An invisible form of matter that does not emit light but has a gravitational effect. It appears to hold galaxies together and bend light around them, but we cannot directly detect it.
一种不发光但有引力效应的不可见物质。它似乎将星系聚合在一起并使光线弯曲，但我们无法直接探测到它。
Dark Energy 暗能量 — A mysterious force that appears to be causing the universe to expand at an ever-increasing rate. Observations of distant supernovae since 1998 suggest this acceleration is real, but its cause is unknown.
一种神秘的力，似乎导致宇宙以越来越快的速度膨胀。自1998年以来对遥远超新星的观测表明这种加速是真实的，但其原因尚不清楚。
Together, dark matter and dark energy are estimated to make up ~95% of the universe — yet we have no direct evidence of what they are. 暗物质和暗能量估计占宇宙的约95%，但我们还没有关于它们本质的直接证据。
